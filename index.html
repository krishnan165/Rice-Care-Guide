Rice Care Guide

Soil for Rice

Well-drained, flooded fields.

Rice plants thrive in well-drained soil, preferably flooded fields for optimal growth and development.

Watering

Frequent flooding and intermittent drying.

Rice requires frequent flooding and intermittent drying of fields to mimic its natural habitat.

Sunlight

Full sun exposure.

Plant rice in an area with full sunlight exposure for optimal photosynthesis and growth.

Benefits

Staple food for many cultures.

Rice is a staple food for a significant portion of the world's population, providing essential nutrients and calories.

Supports diverse ecosystems.

Rice fields can support diverse ecosystems, contributing to biodiversity and environmental health.

Considerations

Requires specific water management practices; avoid waterlogging and ensure proper drainage.
